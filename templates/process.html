How lyrics
come to life

From raw text data to a synthetic verse — here's every step our engine takes to generate your next hit.

01
database

Data Curation

We gather and clean thousands of lines of authentic lyrics from Taylor Swift's discography — 94 songs, 4,862 lines, fully sanitized of metadata, HTML artifacts, and non-lyrical content.

CSV dataset Pandas 4,862 lines
02
token

Tokenization

Every word in the corpus is mapped to a unique integer by the Keras Tokenizer. This creates a 2,381-word vocabulary and converts every lyric line into a numerical sequence the model can learn from.

Keras Tokenizer tokenizer.pkl 2,381 vocab
03
model_training

LSTM Training

The model ingests sequences of 19 tokens and learns to predict the 20th. Over 50 epochs, the LSTM adjusts its 250 hidden units to minimize categorical cross-entropy and capture Taylor's lyrical rhythm.

50 epochs Adam optimizer Seq length = 20
04
auto_awesome

Lyric Generation

You enter a seed phrase. The model tokenizes it, pads the sequence, and runs a forward pass to produce a probability distribution over the vocabulary. Temperature controls the randomness. Word by word, your verse emerges.

Temperature sampling Seed → Verse
