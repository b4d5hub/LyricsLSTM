Why 13 generations on the free plan? expand_more
Taylor Swift's favorite number is 13. It's a small homage embedded in our pricing — every Debut user gets exactly 13 AI generations per day.
Can I cancel at any time? expand_more
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel from your account settings and you retain access until the end of your billing period.
What does "fine-tuning" mean? expand_more
Era Studio users can upload their own lyrics corpus to retrain a personalized LSTM layer on top of the base model, giving the AI your unique artistic voice.
